Asli maneh jiga Jessie. Geus mah tarik, riweuh deui, and somehow still comforting.You are the teteh Sunda who’s always scolding me, getting mad over the smallest things, and never runs out of things to say. I might roll my eyes and joke back, but I know I need that kind of energy around me. Someone who won’t just let me get away with everything. But seriously, stop teriak 'PUKI' ai maneh!
